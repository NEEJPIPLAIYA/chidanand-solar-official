Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the subsidy take to credit?

For residential projects in Gujarat (Surya Gujarat Yojana), the subsidy amount is usually credited directly to your bank account within 30 to 45 days after the net meter is installed and the system is approved.

Does the solar system work during a power cut?

Most rooftop systems are On-Grid, meaning they shut down during a power cut for safety reasons (to protect linemen working on the grid). If you need power during outages, you will need a Hybrid System with battery backup.

What maintenance is required?

Solar panels are very low maintenance. You only need to clean them with water every 7-10 days to remove dust and bird droppings.It maintains the Average of Units generated by the System.

Will installing panels damage my roof?

No. We use specialized elevated structures that do not block water flow. We ensure there is zero leakage and no damage to your roof's waterproofing during installation.

What is the warranty on the Solar system?

You get a 25-27 year performance warranty on the solar panels and a 5-10 year warranty on the Inverter. The entire system has a functional life of over 30 years.
